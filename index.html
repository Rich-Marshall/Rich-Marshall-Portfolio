About Me

Hi I’m Rich! I’m an aspiring UX designer from Bristol and a First Class 2019 Graduate, with a unique skill set including excellent interpersonal skills combined with a strong technical background. I have developed a passion for user experience and have tailored many aspects of my degree, including my Final Year Project, to reflect this.

As captain of my university snowboard team for two years, I took a leadership role and consistently raised the standard of the team and the results we achieved. During this time, DMU SNOW became a family and is full of friends for life (pictured above).

I have a varied history of employment which has helped me to develop my communication and interpersonal skills throughout my life. My CV can be viewed by following the link at the top or bottom of this page.
